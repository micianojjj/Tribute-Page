Monkey D. Luffy

The man who became a pirate king!

As one of the Four Emperors, and at the young age of 19, Luffy is one of the four most powerful pirates in the world.

Luffy's Obtained Haki:

  • Armament Haki - Luffy became an extremely skilled Armament Haki user, having learned it from Silvers Rayleigh during the two-year timeskip.

  • Conqueror's Haki - Luffy is an immensely skilled user of Conqueros's Haki, a very rare type of Haki whose users are said to have the qualities of a king.

  • Observation Haki - Luffy is an extremely skilled user of Observation Haki, which he learned during the two-year timeskip. With it, he is capable of sensing the presence, emotion, and intent of others, greatly aiding his ability to dodge attacks, such as bullets.

    • Luffy's Gear

  • Gear 2 - Luffy increases the speed of his blood flow, and thanks to the rubber nature of his blood vessels and organs, his body can withstand the pressure.

  • Gear 3 - By blowing a massive amount of air into his thumb, Luffy can inflate the bones of a particular part of his body, making it gigantic.

  • Gear 4 - By blowing into his arm, Luffy inflates the muscles in his entire body and covers it with Conqueror's Haki

  • Gear 5(Awakening) - Luffy gains even greater freedom and demonstrates many new abilities such as being able to freely manipulate the shape and size of his body
